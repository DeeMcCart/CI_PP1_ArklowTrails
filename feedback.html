About

Arklow has a rich and varied landscape with coast, riverside, woodland and hilly terrain.


Spectacular views can be seen along the Wicklow and Wexford coastlines, particularly from elevated viewing points.

Locations of historic interest include Arklow Pyramid (actually a burial crypt), remains of the Kynochs gunpowder plant which operated here in the early 1900's, Arklow maritime museum. Walking, hiking and jogging trails have been cultivated to highlight points of interest
